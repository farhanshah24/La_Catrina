Welcome to La Catrina Mexican Bistro

Authentic Flavors, Modern Spirit

Located at 3803 10th Ave in New York City’s vibrant Inwood neighborhood, La Catrina Mexican Bistro brings the soul of Mexico to the heart of Manhattan. Inspired by rich culinary traditions and the festive spirit of La Catrina, our restaurant offers a fresh, modern take on classic Mexican cuisine.
From handmade tortillas and bold, slow-cooked moles to zesty ceviches and craft margaritas, every dish is a tribute to authentic flavors and creative presentation. Whether you’re here for a cozy dinner, weekend brunch, or lively evening with friends, our warm atmosphere and colorful décor make every visit memorable.
Celebrate the taste of Mexico—one plate, one sip, one smile at a time.
